4 Axis Robot

Fusion 360 · Arduino (C++) · Python

What?
A 4-axis robotic arm designed completely from scratch. As a demo, it placed blocks in a tic-tac-toe grid and played full games against a human opponent.

Filtration System
for Remote Communities

Team Leadership · Arduino (C++) · Fusion 360

What?
Led a team of 5 to design a water filtration system for northern communities, targeting over 100L/day of clean drinking water.
How?
Programmed Arduino-controlled chlorination and agitation in C++. Designed and 3D-printed a compact housing for electronics and pumps in Fusion 360.
Results?
Produced drinkable water from heavily soiled sources, exceeding the 100L/day target. Our design was recognized as the top performer in the cohort for both efficiency and reliability.

Automated Culinary Robotics System

C++ · Fusion 360 · LEGO Technic

What?
A robot designed to make custom pudding cups for a quarter by mixing pudding powder, water, and sprinkles. Users could select flavors, and the machine handled the rest.
How?
Programmed user input, sensors, and motor control in C++. Built the machine using LEGO Technic parts and custom 3D-printed components modeled in Fusion 360.

12ft Trebuchet!

Hands-on Design

What?
Built a 12-ft, 200 lb trebuchet capable of handling 150 lb dynamic loads and launching projectiles over 250 ft. The project became a cross-generational effort, designed and assembled together with my grandma.
How?
Recycled wood from a demolished deck and used a mix of hand and power tools to construct the trebuchet, while online simulations helped test arm lengths and counterweight ratios.
Results?
Built a fully functional trebuchet that consistently launched projectiles over 250 ft, showcasing creative problem-solving, resourceful engineering, and effective collaboration.
